Entrepreneurship Curriculum

A 4-year health care entrepreneurship curriculum that moves students from frameworks and fundamentals to prototyping and fundraising.

Venture Ideation & Creation

A 6-month systematic ideation program, composed of teams of 2-4 students, designed to generate patentable IP.

Prototyping Lab

A prototyping lab located in the heart of the Longwood campus, complete with 3D printers, arduino boards, a Microsoft HoloLens, and other hardware.

  • Applying Emerging Technologies

    Teaching and applying the latest advances in 3D printing, augmented reality, and more to health and medicine.


  • Growing Startups in Health Care

    Learn the tools of the trade from entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders.
